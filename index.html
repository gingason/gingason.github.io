Welcome to Manchester United

Manchester United is one of the most famous football clubs in the world. Founded in 1878, the club has a rich history filled with legendary players, major trophies, and passionate fans from around the globe.

This website explores the history of Manchester United, some of its most iconic legends, the trophies the club has won, and a gallery of images celebrating the team.
