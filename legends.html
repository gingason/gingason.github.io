Manchester United Legends

Sir Alex Ferguson

Sir Alex Ferguson is the most successful manager in Manchester United history. He managed the club from 1986 to 2013 and won 13 Premier League titles, 2 UEFA Champions League trophies, and many domestic cups. His leadership helped turn Manchester United into one of the biggest football clubs in the world.

Wayne Rooney

Wayne Rooney is Manchester United's all-time leading goal scorer. He joined the club in 2004 and helped United win multiple Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo developed into one of the best football players in the world during his time at Manchester United and helped the club win several major trophies.

David Beckham

David Beckham was known for his incredible passing ability and free kicks. He was part of Manchester United's famous treble winning team in 1999.
