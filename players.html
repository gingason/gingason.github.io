Legendary Manchester United Players

Wayne Rooney

Wayne Rooney is Manchester United's all-time leading goal scorer. He joined the club in 2004 and quickly became one of the most important players in the team. Rooney helped Manchester United win multiple Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo joined Manchester United in 2003 as a young player and developed into one of the best footballers in the world. During his time at the club, he won the Ballon d'Or and helped the team win the Premier League and Champions League.

David Beckham

David Beckham was famous for his incredible passing ability and free kicks. He was a key part of Manchester United's famous treble-winning team in 1999, which won the Premier League, FA Cup, and Champions League.
